Richard M. Hale Cap & Skull Scholarship

On an annual basis, the Cap & Skull Society awards the Richard M. Hale Cap & Skull Memorial Scholarship to rising juniors exhibiting the qualities of academic excellence and extracurricular involvement early in their college careers regardless of financial need.

To be eligible for the Scholarship, students must achieve a minimum 3.0 cumulative grade point average after the first term of their sophomore year and be enrolled in any undergraduate program at Rutgers, New Brunswick/Piscataway as a full-time student in the School of Arts and Sciences, the Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy, the School of Engineering, or the Mason Gross School of the Arts.
